# Privacy Policy for Neurix

**Last updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

Neurix ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address and authentication data when you sign in with Google or email/password
- **Voice Data**: Voice recordings are processed in real-time for speech-to-text conversion and are not stored on our servers
- **Memories**: Text content you choose to save as memories

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, feature interactions

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Neurix service
- Process your voice commands and queries
- Store and retrieve your memories
- Send reminder notifications
- Improve our services

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- Memories are stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- The app functions offline with local data

### Cloud Storage (Optional)
- If you sign in, your data is synced to Google Firebase/Firestore
- Cloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- You can delete your cloud data at any time

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Authentication**: For user sign-in
- **Cloud Firestore**: For cloud data sync
- **Groq API**: For AI-powered responses (text queries only, no personal data sent)
- **Google Speech Services**: For voice recognition

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:
- All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Authentication tokens are securely stored
- On-device AI processing for intent classification (no data sent to servers)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your memories
- Opt-out of cloud sync by using the app without signing in

## Children's Privacy

Neurix is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
